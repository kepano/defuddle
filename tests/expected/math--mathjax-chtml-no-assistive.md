## Reliability of the joint estimate

We assume that the group computes its joint estimate by averaging the individual estimates:

$$
z = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i = 1}^{n} x_{i}
$$

For a given group size, the standard deviation of the joint estimate is

$$
\sigma_{group} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}
$$

and the precision of the joint estimate is

$$
p_{group} = \frac{1}{\sigma_{group}^{2}}
$$
